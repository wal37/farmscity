Platform overview

The control layer for modern farm operations.

FarmsCity helps farms, cooperatives, extension teams, and agribusiness operators move from scattered notebooks and disconnected spreadsheets into one operational platform built around crops, livestock, weather, records, and networked field visibility.

Planning

Season planning, rotation mapping, planting windows, and activity scheduling.

Monitoring

Daily field updates, livestock health events, rainfall changes, and input usage.

Decision support

Weather-aware actions, crop history, treatment records, and production trends.

Data records

Every decision becomes part of a usable farm record.

FarmsCity keeps the operational memory of the farm network intact. Planting dates, veterinary actions, spray history, rainfall changes, and harvest outcomes can all be reviewed together instead of disappearing into separate files.
